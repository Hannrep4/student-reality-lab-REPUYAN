About This Calculator

This chatbot assistant helps college graduates in New Jersey understand rent affordability. It uses the 30% rule: your monthly rent should not exceed 30% of your gross monthly income. If it does, you may face financial strain. When rent exceeds 40%, you're considered rent-burdened.

Covered NJ Cities: Newark, Jersey City, Paramus, Elizabeth, Hoboken, Trenton, Atlantic City, New Brunswick
